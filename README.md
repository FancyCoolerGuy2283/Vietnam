# MyTV-Vietnam
MyTV is a leading interactive television service in Vietnam, operated by VNPT-Media, a subsidiary of the Vietnam Posts and Telecommunications Group (VNPT). Launched in 2009, it has grown into one of the country's largest IPTV and OTT (Over-the-Top) platforms, offering nearly 200 television channels and a massive library of 70,000 hours.
